A bit about me

Tech Lead & Senior Software Engineer with 8+ years of experience building and leading large-scale web and mobile applications across fintech, healthcare, banking, and Web3.

I’ve led engineering teams of up to 48 people, architected super-app platforms serving millions of users, and delivered systems for clients like NAB, Vietcombank, Thomson Medical, and Prudential. One of my projects was a crypto exchange that ranked in the top 60 on CoinMarketCap.

I specialize in micro-frontend architecture and super-app design — building modular, maintainable platforms where multiple teams can ship independently without stepping on each other’s toes.

Beyond coding, I focus on engineering excellence: CI/CD pipelines, code quality standards, and mentoring engineers to grow into stronger developers.
